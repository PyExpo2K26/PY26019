    SCS Curve Number Reference Table

    The SCS Curve Number method (USDA TR-55) converts rainfall to direct runoff based on land use and soil type. Higher CN = more runoff, less infiltration.

    Hydrologic Soil Groups
    A — Sandy soils, high infiltration (low runoff)
    B — Loamy soils, moderate infiltration
    C — Clay-loam soils, slow infiltration
    D — Clay soils, very slow infiltration (high runoff)
    SCS Formula
    S = (25400/CN) − 254
    Ia = 0.2 × S
    Q = (P − Ia)² / (P − Ia + S)
    where P=rainfall, Q=runoff, S=max retention
